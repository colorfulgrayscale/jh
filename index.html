For me, great architecture should encompass more than programmatic, aesthetic and structural concerns. Social responsibility and sustainability must become priorities for Architecture and Urban Design. A serious shift towards sustainable attitudes combined with a healthy respect for the environment should permeate the creation of architectural artifacts. The research, use and development of low-cost, sustainable materials would enable all classes of society to experience the dignity, beauty and comfort of good design. Architecture and Urban Design should not only affect quality of life, but also the cost of living. It should excite emotions, evoke positive social change and preserve the environment for future generations.
The great thing about being an architect is that you can walk into your dreams.

Harold E. Wagoner, 1986
